Request Data Deletion

Infinite Labyrinth

Effective Date: February 27, 2026

Infinite Labyrinth is published by Trinath Sai Subhash Reddy Pittala. This app does not use user accounts. The following explains what data the app may store and how you can request that we remove it.

Data we may store

  • On your device (local): High scores, game progress, and settings (e.g. sound preferences). This data stays on your device only.
  • On our servers (cloud): If you use leaderboard features, we may store game scores and an anonymous identifier in Google Firebase (Cloud Firestore). We do not store your name, email, or phone number.

Steps to request deletion

  1. Local data: To delete all data stored on your device, clear the app’s data in your device settings, or uninstall Infinite Labyrinth. No request to us is needed.
  2. Cloud data: To request deletion of any data we hold in the cloud (e.g. leaderboard entries), email us at subhash8111999@gmail.com. Include “Infinite Labyrinth – data deletion” in the subject line and, if possible, any identifier or details that help us find your data (e.g. nickname or score you used on the leaderboard).
  3. We will process your request and confirm once the data has been removed.

Contact: subhash8111999@gmail.com

What we delete and what we keep

  • Deleted: All game data we hold that is linked to your request (scores, progress, anonymous identifiers). We do not keep copies after deletion.
  • Kept: Nothing beyond what is needed to process your request (e.g. we may keep your email in our inbox for a short period for support purposes only).

How long until data is removed

We will complete deletion requests within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once your data has been removed. Local data is deleted as soon as you clear app data or uninstall the app.
